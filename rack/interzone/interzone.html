User Guide

The major controls of Interzone have CV inputs available allowing for full automation of those parameters.

VCO

Interzone features one voltage controlled oscillator (VCO) that outputs 3 waveforms simultaneously: Saw, Pulse and a Sub wave. These waves are mixed using the mixer section discussed below

The VCO section in Interzone is where you control the tuning, pulse width, and modulation applied to the VCO's pitch and pulse width.
