About

All software produced by Valley Audio is programmed and tested by me, Dale Johnson. I have a passion to create new and exciting audio effects, processors and synthesizers.

What's in the name?

'Valley' is a literal translation of my name 'Dale', a word that historically meant 'an open valley'. The most famous usage of the word is 'The Yorkshire Dales', which as an area in northern England that is known for its stunning scenery and rolling landscape.
